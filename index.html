About Jeanie

Jeanie is your personal AI assistant, available 24/7 through a simple phone call. Whether you need directions to the nearest pharmacy, want to check what's playing at your local theatre, or need help with daily reminders, Jeanie is here to help with just a conversation. No apps, no complicated technology – just your voice and a phone line.

We believe everyone deserves access to AI assistance, regardless of their technical expertise or device limitations. That's why we created Jeanie to work through the most natural form of communication – voice. By making advanced technology accessible through any phone, from basic landlines to smartphones, we're building a more inclusive digital future where powerful AI assistance is just a call away. As we continue to develop and improve, every conversation with Jeanie helps us create a service that truly works for everyone, everywhere.
